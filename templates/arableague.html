The Arab League, formally the League of Arab States, is an intergovernmental organisation of 22 Arab nations in North Africa and the Middle East, aiming to strengthen relations, coordinate policies, and cooperate on various issues, including political, economic, and cultural matters. The Arab League & Observers is a specially formed committee towards the end of the First Arab-Israeli War, specifically the War for Israeli Independence, to determine a conclusion to the War in the form of an Armistice.
